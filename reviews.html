"One of the Best Breakfast I have had."

Pamela's Diner has been a staple of the Strip District and other neighborhoods throughout the city. They have the most incredible lyonnaise potatoes, hot cakes, breakfast varieties for all. I highly recommend this Strip District location just for the historical photographs and ambience of the 50's style diner. Definitely make sure you come early because they are packed on the weekends! They do have outdoor seating when the weather is nice which is so great for people watching! And it's so great to be outside dining. Great service quick & efficient.
